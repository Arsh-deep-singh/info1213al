Rules and Regulation

As every game Kabaddi also has its own rules. All of them arelisted below:
  • The overall object of the game is to simply score more points than the opposition team within the allotted time.
  • Kabaddi is played by two teams that both consist of twelve players each. Out of which only seven players will play on mat.
  • Kabaddi has different age and weight categories as it requires a lot of physical strength.
  • There are two halves of 20 minutes. The team which scores more points wins the game.
  • In order to score point the raider have to touch any opponent and come back to his half while holding his breath.
  • Catcher has to stop the raider from returning back to his half.
  • Each team will take their turn as raider and catcher throughout the 40 minutes.
